The Overview

Over the years my work has focused primarily on websites and applications. By merit of focusing on small businesses, most of those projects are no longer live. To get a sense for my design approach, take a close look at this and the website for Three Pines Ventures. My design style tends to focus on "what would be the most approachable to a person on the Autism Spectrum." And thus focuses on minimalistic approach with high color contrasts and soft tones. Or to put it bluntly. "I want you to be relaxed while using this, not stressed because of it."
